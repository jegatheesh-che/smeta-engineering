WHO WE ARE

Smeta Engineering Services Private Limited (SESPL) is an ISO 9001:2015 certified engineering process outsourcing (EPO) firm with its headquarters in India.

SESPL specializes in providing design, engineering, bidding support (pre-contract quantity survey, estimation and costing) services to civil, mechanical, electrical, instrumentation and MEP contractors.

SESPL is formed by engineers experienced in various disciplines and fields of the construction industry.

SESPL acts as a back-end engineering and bidding support team to contracting organizations in the construction business worldwide.

SESPL can also assist engineering consultants, architects, project management consultants, professional quantity surveyors and estimators to prepare any kind of engineering and tender documentation.

We offer a variety of services to our clients including:

BIDDING SUPPORT

Smeta Engineering Services Private Limited (SESPL) provides tailor made bidding support services to suit your requirements. Our team will work as an integral part of your organization and not as an external agency. SESPL can also assist your existing tendering team when time is a constraint to submit the bids.

DESIGN & ENGINEERING

Smeta Engineering Services Private Limited offers reliable and cost effective design & engineering solutions meeting client’s specifications, local standards & regulations.
